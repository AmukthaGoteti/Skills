# NAS - Network Analysis and Synthesis

## Overview

This repository contains notes, assignments, circuit analyses, simulations, and implementations related to **Network Analysis and Synthesis (NAS)**. It covers the fundamental principles of electrical network theory, circuit analysis techniques, transient and steady-state responses, and network synthesis methods used in electronics and communication engineering.

---

## Topics Covered

* Two Port Networks
* DC Tranient Analysis (1^st and 2^nd Order Circuits)
* Locus Diagrams & Resonance
* Network Functions
* Synthesis of One Port Networks

## Learning Outcomes

By completing the material in this repository, you will be able to:

* Analyze DC and AC electrical networks.
* Apply network theorems to simplify complex circuits.
* Solve transient and steady-state circuit problems.
* Analyze circuits using Laplace transforms.
* Design and synthesize passive electrical networks.
* Understand two-port network parameters and applications.
